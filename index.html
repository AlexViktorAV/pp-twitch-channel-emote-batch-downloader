Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. The Twitch Channel Emote Batch Downloader extension is designed to operate locally within your browser.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, user activity, or browsing history. All operations, including emote fetching and ZIP compression, are performed locally on your device.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Downloads: To trigger the automatic download of the ZIP file to your computer.
  • Host Permissions (static-cdn.jtvnw.net): Necessary to retrieve emote image files from Twitch's content delivery network.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the support link on the Chrome Web Store.
